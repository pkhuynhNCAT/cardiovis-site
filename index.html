Our Solutions

Three Core Innovations

Comprehensive solutions designed to transform cardiac valve surgery through advanced AI and computer vision

AUTO-VALVE Portal

Data Platform

A secure edge-to-cloud platform that unifies multimodal cardiac surgery data, enabling seamless integration of imaging and video streams with automated annotation capabilities.

  • Multimodal data unification (CT, TEE, video)
  • Automatic HIPAA-compliant de-identification
  • Real-time synchronization and annotation
  • Web-based collaborative platform
Learn more

Computer Vision for Surgery

AI Intelligence

Real-time surgical intelligence that analyzes operative video to track procedures, identify instruments and anatomy, and automatically generate training materials.

  • Automatic phase and tool detection
  • Real-time procedural guidance
  • Automated teaching clip generation
  • Post-operative analytics and summaries
Learn more

Digital Twin Technology

Predictive Modeling

Patient-specific virtual heart valve models built from routine imaging, enabling surgeons to preview and optimize repair strategies before surgery.

  • 3D reconstruction from TEE/CT imaging
  • Interactive surgical planning scenarios
  • Outcome prediction and optimization
  • Educational visualization tools
