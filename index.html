AUTO-VALVE Portal

Comprehensive Data Integration Platform

The AUTO-VALVE Portal represents a breakthrough in surgical data management, providing seamless integration of multimodal cardiac surgery data streams. Our platform unifies CT imaging, 2D/3D transesophageal echocardiography (TEE), and endoscopic/robotic video feeds into a single, coherent system that maintains perfect temporal synchronization across all modalities.

Key Capabilities

  • Automatic HIPAA-compliant de-identification ensuring patient privacy while enabling research
  • Clock-true time synchronization across all data streams for accurate event correlation
  • Clean web UI/API supporting data ingestion, search, streaming, and collaborative annotation
  • Advanced computer vision hooks for automatic annotation of surgical phases, instruments, and anatomy
  • Standardized ontologies ensuring reproducible benchmarks and consistent model training
  • Edge-to-cloud architecture enabling both local processing and cloud-based analytics

Clinical Benefits

By centralizing and standardizing surgical data, AUTO-VALVE enables unprecedented insights into surgical performance, patient outcomes, and best practices. Surgeons can review cases with perfect multimodal alignment, researchers can access curated datasets for AI development, and institutions can benchmark their performance against anonymized global standards.

Computer Vision for Cardiac Surgery

Real-Time Surgical Intelligence

Our computer vision system acts as an intelligent observer in the operating room, continuously analyzing operative video and TEE imaging to provide real-time insights and documentation. The system understands surgical context, tracks procedural progress, and identifies critical anatomical structures and instruments without any manual input.

Advanced Features

  • Automatic phase detection identifying current procedural step with 98.5% accuracy
  • Real-time instrument tracking and usage analytics for efficiency optimization
  • Anatomical structure recognition with automated labeling and measurement
  • Step-by-step timing analytics comparing against benchmarks and best practices
  • Automatic generation of teaching clips highlighting key techniques and decision points
  • Comprehensive post-case summaries including time-in-step metrics and performance indicators

Educational Impact

The system transforms every surgery into a learning opportunity by automatically creating annotated teaching materials, identifying exemplary techniques, and providing objective performance metrics. This enables more effective training, faster skill acquisition, and continuous improvement across surgical teams.

Intra-operative AI Copilot

Multimodal Large Language Model Assistant

Our AI Copilot represents the next generation of surgical decision support, combining advanced language models with real-time analysis of operative video, TEE imaging, vital signs, and robot kinematics. The system provides contextual guidance while maintaining complete surgeon autonomy and control.

Intelligent Assistance Features

  • Real-time procedural awareness identifying current surgical phase and next steps
  • Contextual instrument and anatomy identification with relevant clinical information
  • Evidence-based recommendations with clear source citations from guidelines and literature
  • Safety checklist integration with gentle reminders at critical decision points
  • Natural language query support for instant access to guidelines and best practices
  • Surgeon-controlled confirmation workflow ensuring human oversight of all suggestions

Safety and Compliance

The AI Copilot operates strictly as a decision support system, never controlling instruments or making autonomous decisions. Every recommendation is traceable to its source material, whether from clinical guidelines, device IFUs, or peer-reviewed literature. The system maintains a complete audit trail of all interactions for quality assurance and continuous improvement.

Digital Twin for Cardiac Surgery

Patient-Specific Virtual Heart Modeling

Our Digital Twin technology creates precise, patient-specific virtual models of heart valves from routine imaging data. These interactive 3D models enable surgeons to explore repair options, predict outcomes, and optimize surgical strategies before entering the operating room.

Advanced Simulation Capabilities

  • Automatic 3D reconstruction from standard TEE or CT imaging data
  • Interactive "what-if" scenarios for different repair strategies and techniques
  • Ring size optimization with predicted coaptation and hemodynamic outcomes
  • Chord length and positioning analysis for optimal repair geometry
  • Leaflet resection planning with visualization of resulting valve configuration
  • Pressure gradient and flow pattern predictions based on computational fluid dynamics

Clinical Decision Support

The Digital Twin provides quantitative metrics for surgical planning including expected coaptation surface area, predicted regurgitation risk, and estimated pressure gradients. Surgeons can compare multiple repair strategies side-by-side, share plans with colleagues for consultation, and use the models for patient education and informed consent discussions.

AI-Enhanced Robotics for Cardiac Surgery

Intelligent Robotic Assistance Layer

Our AI enhancement layer adds intelligent capabilities to existing robotic platforms like the Intuitive da Vinci system. By analyzing real-time video, kinematics, and surgical context, the system provides assistance that reduces cognitive load while maintaining complete surgeon control.

Smart Assistance Features

  • Camera autopilot automatically centering the surgical field and maintaining optimal views
  • Horizon leveling and field-of-view optimization reducing the need for manual adjustments
  • Proximity warnings for critical structures like chordae and papillary muscles
  • Soft "no-go" zones preventing inadvertent contact with sensitive anatomy
  • Suture planning assistance suggesting optimal bite locations and spacing
  • Ring sizing recommendations based on valve geometry analysis
  • Tremor filtration and motion scaling for enhanced precision

Platform Integration

Our system integrates seamlessly with existing robotic platforms through ROS 2 compatibility, supporting both research systems like the dVRK and clinical platforms like the da Vinci Xi/X. The assistance features can be customized to surgeon preferences and gradually introduced as comfort levels increase, ensuring smooth adoption and maximum benefit.
